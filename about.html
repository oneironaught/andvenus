Who we are











And Venus is an up-and-coming band hailing from the vibrant music scene of Weslaco, Texas. The band members, consist of lead vocalist and guitarist Robert Ruelas, bassist Adolfo Perez, drummer Fernando Cruz, and lead guitarist Nathaniel Garcia.

With influences ranging from classic rock legends like Led Zeppelin and Pink Floyd to modern indie icons such as
The Mars Volta and Incubus, And Venus has crafted a sound that is both nostalgic and fresh. Their music is characterized by catchy guitar riffs, memorable lyrics, and a driving rhythm section that never fails to get the crowd moving.

Since their formation in 2007, And Venus has been steadily building a dedicated fanbase through their electrifying
live performances and engaging online presence. Their songs, which often explore themes of love, loss, and the psychological complexities of human relationships, resonate deeply with their listeners.
