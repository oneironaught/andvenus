    • Who we are













And Venus is an up-and-coming band hailing from the vibrant music scene of Weslaco, Texas. The band members, consisting of lead vocalist and guitarist Robert Ruelas, bassist Adolfo Perez, drummer Fernando Cruz, and lead Nathaniel Garcia.

With influences ranging from classic rock legends like Led Zeppelin and Pink Floyd to modern indie icons such as The Mars Volta and Incubus, And Venus has crafted a sound that is both nostalgic and fresh. Their music is characterized by catchy guitar riffs, memorable lyrics, and a driving rhythm section that never fails to get the crowd moving.

Since their formation in 2007, And Venus has been steadily building a dedicated fanbase through their electrifying live performances and engaging online presence. Their songs, which often explore themes of love, loss, and the psychological complexities of human relationships, resonate deeply with their listeners.

As they continue to grow and evolve, And Venus remains committed to creating music that is both authentic and accessible. With their infectious energy and undeniable talent, this band is poised to make a lasting impact on the music world.
