Who we are

And Venus is a quartet based out of Weslaco, TX that embodies a lively and eclectic sound, ranging from complex rhythms and odd time signatures to elegant poetic devices that will ensnare you after the first listen. They began their musical journey in early 2009 relocating from Weslaco, TX to Austin. And Venus is Fernando Cruz (Drums), Adolfo Perez (Bass), Nate Garcia (Guitar/Processing), and Bobby Ruelas (Vocals/Guitar).
